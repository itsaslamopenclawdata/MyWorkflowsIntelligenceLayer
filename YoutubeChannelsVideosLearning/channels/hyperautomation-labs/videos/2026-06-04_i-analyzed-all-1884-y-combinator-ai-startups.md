Five funded patterns emerge: (1) B2B crushes consumer 67% vs <9% (≈8:1), (2) within B2B, ~20% are picks-and-shovels tools for builders, the other 80% are vertical — one boring expensive workflow per industry, (3) "agent" is the new pitch word (4% in 2018 → >25% in 2026 batches), (4) median team is 5 people, 73% have ≤10, only 3% are solo — sweet spot is 2–3 co-founders, (5) SF still dominant (59%) but 45% are remote-friendly.
