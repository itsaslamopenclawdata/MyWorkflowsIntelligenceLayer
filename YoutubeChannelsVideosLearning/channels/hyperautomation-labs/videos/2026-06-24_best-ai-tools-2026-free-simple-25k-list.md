## Key Insights

- **No single AI assistant wins in 2026 — smart people use more than one.** ChatGPT is the best all-rounder, Claude is the smartest on hard problems and writes most like a human, and Gemini is the best free option baked right into Google. Practical advice: run Claude and ChatGPT side-by-side on the same prompt for a week and pick your favorite.
- **Vibe coding is now a mainstream category that barely existed 10 months ago.** Tools like Lovable let non-developers ship working apps (with database, login, payments) from a one-sentence description. Claude Code is the serious developer pick for terminal-native building. The "agency-thousands" use case has been compressed to "one sentence."
- **Dedicated AI writing apps are mostly dead.** Chat models got so good they ate the whole category — Claude is the best writer for 90% of people (human-sounding, AI-detector resistant), with ChatGPT a close second. The trick is prompt framing: assign a persona and an audience instead of saying "write me a post."
- **Midjourney got dethroned in images.** Google's Nano Banana Pro (in the Gemini app) is the new king — best at realism, character consistency across images, and the only model that writes correct text *inside* an image, making it killer for thumbnails/posters/ads. Free with daily limits.
- **Sora is dead — Veo is the new video king.** OpenAI shut down the Sora app in April 2026. Google's Veo (via Google Flow) generates 4K short clips from a sentence and is the only tool that adds matching sound automatically. Kling is the best free-credit alternative.
- **Search has become an assistant, not a list of links.** Perplexity answers in plain language with sources cited on every sentence, and its Comet browser can read your tabs and execute tasks. Research moved from "10 blue links" to "agent that does the work."
- **Automation split by skill level.** Zapier (free tier covers 100 tasks/month) for non-technical users; n8n (free if self-hosted) exploded this year as the builder choice for wiring up real AI agents.
- **Three new categories since the original viral post:** AI browsers (Comet, ChatGPT Atlas) that read pages and do tasks; computer-use agents (Claude's CoSpace) that click around your screen and execute work; and vibe coding (Lovable, Claude Code) that builds software from English descriptions.
- **Original list got 2/12 right.** Suno (music) and Fireflies (meeting notes) survive. Fireflies now faces competition from Granola (quiet background listening, no awkward bot) and Fathom (best fully free option).
