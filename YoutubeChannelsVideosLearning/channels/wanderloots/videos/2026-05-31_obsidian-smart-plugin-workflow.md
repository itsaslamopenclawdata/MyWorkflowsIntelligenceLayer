## TL;DR

A 25-minute walkthrough of the updated Smart Plugins in Obsidian using the "Smart Loop" with Smart Connections, Smart Context, and Smart Chat — Discover, Bundle, Use. Smart Connections is a free, private, local AI tool that runs inside Obsidian and uses a local embeddings model (NOT an LLM) to do semantic search over your notes. The newer updates add Smart Context (auto-updating context bundles) and Smart Chat (passes that context to AI).
