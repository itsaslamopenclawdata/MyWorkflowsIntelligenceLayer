  - **Detection:** `ebookgen_setup_wizard.py` SMTP test step logs `❌ SMTP authentication failed`; `/tmp/ebookgen_test.log` shows the same after Step 6.

  - **Detection:** Hermes runs `curl -sL -o /dev/null -w "%{http_code}\n" "<posted thread URL>"` at the +15min and +60min marks; a `404` or `403` from Reddit = removed.

- **Failure: Stripe poll cron fails silently (script crashes mid-run).**
  - **Detection:** `/var/log/ebookgen_poll.log` does not get a new line in 10 minutes after cron scheduled time.
  - **Auto-recovery:** subagent 4 runs `tail -n 50 /var/log/ebookgen_poll.log`, identifies the exception, patches the bug, restarts the cron.
