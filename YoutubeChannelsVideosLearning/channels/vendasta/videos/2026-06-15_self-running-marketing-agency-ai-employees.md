## TL;DR
The split happening in real time across the 66,000-agency Vendasta network: agencies running AI as a workforce (50–80% margins, 5x retainers, 10x output) are leaving agencies running AI as a cheap intern (15–20% margins, same content as everyone else) in the dust. The framework: structure every client as 6 specialist AI employees (ranking, content writer, booking, review-reply, social post, analytics) instead of one generalist. The mindset shift: AI is the engine, not a shortcut — agencies that prompt the same tools the same way produce the same content and become invisible.
