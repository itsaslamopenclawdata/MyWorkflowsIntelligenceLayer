## TL;DR

A solo founder walks through a no-code, end-to-end build of a WhatsApp AI agent in ~15 minutes. Two paths are shown: (1) the easy **WATI** route for a hosted chat-only agent, and (2) the more powerful **WATI + n8n** route that lets the agent take real actions (book Google Calendar slots, send confirmations, hand off to Gmail, etc.). Both start from the same Meta prerequisites (Meta Business Manager + Facebook Page + WhatsApp Business app) and end with a working agent that replies in English and Hindi. Bonus: same flow can be used to broadcast campaigns to thousands of opted-in numbers. Full DOC and an exportable n8n workflow are given away for free on his site.
